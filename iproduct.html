The ARK

The Automated Remote Keyboard, ARK, is one of three keyboards in the cutting-edge lineup manufactured with the current evolving world of IT in mind, our keyboards will grow with the world and its users. The ARK, as its name suggests, specializes in remote access and automated typing, a concept that has been around for a very long time, but the tech has never allowed for the function to exist until now. With the new Peer Pressure Pistons (PPP) the ARK becomes the most customizable keyboard on the planet, perfect for both amateurs and professionals.

PPP keys

The recent breakthrough in keyboard tech, the PPP keys are enhanced with metal hooks, vastly increasing the potential uses of the keys. Its uses include softening the keystroke which reduces stress on the keys, in turn increasing the longevity of the keys by up to 90 million keystrokes, and giving the user control over the keys via the adaptable hooks. This control can reduce, or increase, noise, modifying actuation force and even locking keys in place. The keys themselves support both metal and plastic casings and can be customized to the user's preferences on purchase.

FEU system

A system tailor-made to be used with PPP keys, the application can be downloaded to any PC or mobile device and controls all the functionalities you might want with a PPP keyboard. The app enables the heavy customization the PPP keys have a capacity for and using it you can freely edit any single key, or the entire keyboard, with just a few presses. FEU is not limited to just customizing however, it can actively control the keyboard. While this system has a near infinite amount of uses some popular examples include real-time streaming, virtual couch play and just general remote use.
