Our main product line is our tri-keyboard setup that is bound to change your life for the better whichever one you may choose.

Our team

We are a team of four uprising creators from Sweden, eager to show the world what we burn for with the capabilities to match our ambitions. We aren't looking to save the world, or even a city, but we are actively working towards making the life of everyone in the modern world more exciting and just a little easier. We started from zero, from scratch, in a room full of other creative minds, and got together under the name 'section'. Initially we had no clue what we were getting ourselves into, but as we worked together we slowly realised what made each individual work, how *we* worked. Together, we thrived, coming up with idea after idea, taking one step higher for every hour that passed, there was never a minute where an idea wasn't being put up or worked on. This process has not stopped even now, and we are continously evolving and coming up with new ideas, you're welcome to join on our journey, welcome to our home! The place where we come together and help everyone to a greater daily life in a digital society.
