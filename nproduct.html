PPP keys

The new breakthrough in Keyboard tech, mechanical switches are enhanced with metal hooks, softening the keystroke to reduce stress on the keys to increase key stroke life span by up to 90 million strokes. That's not all, the highly adaptable hooks enable never-before-seen control of the individual keys of the entire keyboard. This can reduce noise, increase or decrease actuation force and even lock keys in place if they should be unable to be accessed or hidden within the keyboard (Perfect for travelling).
